How is my API key stored?

Your key is kept in this extension's own private browser storage. It isn't visible to Splose's web pages, other extensions, or synced anywhere — it stays on this machine. Browser extensions don't have access to the operating system's encrypted keychain, so this is the strongest protection available within a plain Chrome extension. If a centrally-managed, revocable-key approach becomes important later (e.g. many clinician installs), that's a sign to introduce a small backing service rather than relying purely on browser storage.
